Common Questions

Everything you need to know about scheduling, pricing, and warranties.

How quickly can a technician arrive?

We arrive within 2 hours on average. With our same-day emergency dispatch trucks fully stocked, we aim to resolve residential repair tickets on the very first visit.

Do you offer warranties on parts and labor?

Yes. All components (springs, cables, rollers, openers) carry manufacture-backed product warranties, and all ProLift labor services are covered by our own workmanship warranties (up to 1 year on Elite plans).

What garage door brands do you work with?

We work with and service all major manufacturers, including LiftMaster, Chamberlain, Genie, Sears, Clopay, Amarr, Wayne Dalton, and many others. If parts exist, our team can repair it.

Can I get a same-day installation?

While repair runs are resolved same-day, full-door panel replacements depend on custom sizing, material, and insulation availability. We do maintain a massive warehouse of standard options to speed up install times.

Do you service commercial properties?

Yes. ProLift has a dedicated commercial team fully certified to repair, inspect, and install high-frequency industrial openers, warehouse rolling panels, and safety fire doors.
