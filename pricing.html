Pricing FAQ

Clarifications on our service call fee waivers, plan upgrades, and discounts.

Is the service call fee waived if I book a repair?

Yes. When you authorize our technician to proceed with any recommended repair work, the standard service call diagnostic fee is waived entirely from your final bill.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership?

Absolutely. You can modify your membership plan, upgrade to Elite for emergency storm response, or cancel your renewal at any time directly through your customer dashboard with zero penalties.

Are parts included in the membership?

Under the Basic plan, parts are extra. Under the Pro plan, you receive direct wholesale discount access (parts at cost). Under the Elite plan, residential replacement components are fully included up to a value of $500 per service calendar year.

Do you offer senior or military discounts?

Yes. We proudly support local veterans, seniors, and active responders. We offer a 10% discount on all standard repair services and new residential installations.
